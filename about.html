Muganda Straight Up

I'm Henrie Mbogo, born on August 17, 1993, in the heart of Kyambogo, Kampala. A proud Muganda who's spent 33 years mastering the roads of Uganda.

Growing up in Kyambogo taught me resilience, community, and the value of hard work. From the bustling streets of Kampala to the serene countryside, I've driven it all. Every pothole, every shortcut, every hidden gem – I know them like the back of my hand.

But I'm more than just a driver. I'm a son, a friend, and a proud Ugandan who believes in connecting people through safe and reliable transportation. Whether it's taking a bride to her wedding, delivering goods to a business, or showing tourists the beauty of Uganda, I put my heart into every journey.
